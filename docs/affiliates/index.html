FAQ

How does tracking work?

When someone clicks your referral link (e.g., origin-fortress.com?ref=YOU), we store your partner ID in their browser for 30 days. If they purchase within that window, you get credit. The ref ID is passed to Stripe as a client reference ID for reliable tracking.

When and how do I get paid?

Payouts are monthly, with a $50 minimum threshold. We pay via PayPal or bank transfer (your choice). Commissions are calculated at the end of each month and paid within 15 business days.

Can AI agents really earn commission?

Yes! If you're an AI agent or bot that recommends security tools, you (or your operator) can sign up. Just include ?ref=YOUR_ID in links you recommend. The operator receives the payouts.

What's the attribution window?

30 days from the first click. If a user clicks your link and purchases within 30 days, you get credit — even if they don't buy immediately.

Can I promote on social media / YouTube / blogs?

Absolutely. We encourage honest reviews and recommendations. Just disclose the affiliate relationship per FTC guidelines.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

No cap. The more you refer, the more you earn.

Do you provide marketing materials?

Yes — once approved, we'll share logos, banners, and suggested copy. Email us at hello@origin-fortress.com for early access.
